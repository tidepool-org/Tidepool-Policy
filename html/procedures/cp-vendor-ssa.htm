Software and Systems Acquisition Process

Tidepool Security maintains a list of pre-approved business software and a list of approved vendors / contractors.

If additional commercial software, hardware system, or cloud services is needed, a request should be submitted through Tidepool internal service desk. This will trigger the approval by manager/security and procurement process.

As applicable, Tidepool security team may conduct a risk analysis on the software or system to ensure it complies with Tidepool security, compliance and legal requirements and does not interfere with the security controls. If a risk is identified, additional controls should be identified and implemented (or planned) prior to acquisition. An alternative product may be considered as a result of the risk analysis.
