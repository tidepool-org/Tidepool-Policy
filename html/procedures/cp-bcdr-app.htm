Application Service Event Recovery

Tidepool will develop a status page to provide real time update and inform our customers of the status of each service. The status page is updated with details about an event that may cause service interruption / downtime.

A follow up root-cause analysis details (RCA) will be available to customers upon request after the event has transpired for further details to cause and remediation plan for the future.

Event Service Level

Short (hours)

  • Experience a short delay in service.
  • Tidepool will monitor the event and determine course of action. Escalation may be required.
  • Tidepool will notify customers of delay in service and provide updates on Tidepool’s status page.

Moderate (days)

  • Experience a modest delay in service where processes in flight may need to be restarted.
  • Tidepool will monitor the event and determine course of action. Escalation may be required.
  • Tidepool will notify customers of delay in service and provide updates on Tidepool’s status page.

Long (a week or more)

  • Experience a delay in service and processes in flight may need to be restarted.
  • Tidepool will monitor the event and determine course of action. Escalation may be required.
  • Tidepool will notify customers of delay in service and provide updates on Tidepool’s status page.
